Welcome to CricJharkhand

CricJharkhand is the official website of the Jharkhand State Cricket Association (JSCA), the governing body of cricket in the state of Jharkhand. CricJharkhand aims to promote and develop the game of cricket in Jharkhand and provide a platform for the talented players to showcase their skills and represent the state at various levels.

CricJharkhand provides the latest news, updates, fixtures, results, statistics, photos, and videos of the Jharkhand cricket team and its players. CricJharkhand also features the information about the JSCA, its history, vision, mission, objectives, office bearers, committees, members, affiliates, and facilities.

CricJharkhand invites you to explore the website and join us in supporting the Jharkhand cricket team and its players. CricJharkhand is your one-stop destination for everything related to cricket in Jharkhand.
